Shawn Mendes is a Canadian pop singer and songwriter. After uploading a series of his cover versions to various video-sharing sites Mendes earned a dedicated following that he cultivated through social media outlets. His talent, teen-idol good looks and built-in fan base quickly landed him a record deal. Awards

Mr.Mendes has won more than 121 awards and 276 nominations during his career.These include American Music Awards(AMA's),Billboard Music Awards,Brit Awards etc.He has also got 3 nominations for Grammy's in the years 2019 and 2020.
